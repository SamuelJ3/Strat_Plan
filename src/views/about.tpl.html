ABOUT 5-Year Strategic Plan


Welcome to the Systems 5-Year Strategic Vision and Goals. These goals are the result of collaboration across Info Tech. Grounded in the core values and principles that have driven our success for over 40 years this is a living document that will help guide our path forward.

Our Vision details the vision for the Info Tech Systems business, including the historical context and ecosystem factors on which it is based.

Our Goals refer to each division’s 5-year Goal. This goal is the division’s primary 5-year aspirational focus. Each Goal has strategic Objectives through which the division will advance toward their Goal. Each Objective has the Success Measures by which we will know the Objective has been reached. These Goals and Objectives are the foundation from which our annual division, department, units, and individual goals cascade.

The Why that drives our vision and goals is the world we believe we should create.

Aligned with the goals we have included two diagrams that are meant to provide a holistic view of our market construction infrastructure ecosystem and product ecosystem.

If you have questions about your division’s Goal, Objectives, or Success Measures, please contact your Director or Vice President. For general questions and feedback about the Vision and Goals, please contact Bob DeHoff, Director for Strategy and Innovation.
