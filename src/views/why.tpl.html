WHY Our world is driven by Info Tech


Through targeted approaches, we will grow our market, remain people-focused and make the world a better place.

We created our market and plan to keep growing it. We are a homegrown company, and we won’t forget where we came from as we embark on a growth strategy. We will continue to steadfastly support DOTs, AASHTO and their products. Plus, we will increase our drive to provide contractors and consultants with the best tools they need for their businesses and their work with owners and agencies. When we achieve this, we will not only grow, but will be in a position to support the entire infrastructure construction ecosystem between owners, contractors and consultants, offering solutions that ensure value promised is value delivered.

As we grow, our people will remain at the core of our success. We didn’t get to today based on our ideas alone but through the power of collaboration. And, we won’t get to the future without our committed, talented family of employees. We value people first. We believe in investing in people. We view our customers as an extension of our family. We pride ourselves on building long-lasting, authentic relationships and treating people with respect. And, we live for the people who help us achieve the goals of our customers and those of our own.

Our goal is to make the world a better place. We create tools and technology that make things easier for those who build, own, operate and maintain our country’s infrastructure. Our initial software was created to ensure fair and free competition in a competitive market, and still to this day, our solutions protect and highlight truth because we believe that will have the greatest impact on our world.

Ultimately, we will deliver a drive only Info Tech can build. The world is driven by technology, and the technology that builds our world is driven by Info Tech.
